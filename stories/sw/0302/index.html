Moto Fire Moto

Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Matteo E. Mwita

Kiswahili

Level 1

Angalia, moto!

Look, fire!

Angalia, moto!

Moto unachoma.

Fire burns.

Moto unachoma.

Moto unatumika kupika.

Fire cooks.

Moto unatumika kupika.

Moto unaleta joto.

Fire gives warmth.

Moto unaleta joto.

Moto unaleta mwanga.

Fire gives light.

Moto unaleta mwanga.

Angalia, moto!

Look, fire!

Angalia, moto!

Moto ni wa ajabu.

Fire is wonderful.

Moto ni wa ajabu.

Na una nguvu sana.

And it is powerful.

Na una nguvu sana.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Matteo E. Mwita
Language: Kiswahili
Level 1
