Sare za shule School clothes

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini

Gladys Njoki, Racheal Njoki

Kiswahili

Level 1

Rinda hili ni refu.

This dress is long.

Rinda hili ni refu.

Sweta hii ni kubwa.

This jersey is big.

Sweta hii ni kubwa.

Mkoba huu ni mkubwa.

This bag is big.

Mkoba huu ni mkubwa.

Mshipi huu...

This belt is...

Mshipi huu...

Kofia hii ni ndogo.

This hat is small.

Kofia hii ni ndogo.

Sokisi hizi ni fupi.

The socks are short.

Sokisi hizi ni fupi.

Lakini viatu hivi ni vipya.

But these shoes are new.

Lakini viatu hivi ni vipya.

....vinanitosha vizuri.

... they fit well.

....vinanitosha vizuri.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini
Translated by: Gladys Njoki, Racheal Njoki
