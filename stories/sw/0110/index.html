{% include picturecol.html index="02" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Katika kijiji kimoja kwenye mteremko wa Mlima Kenya, katika Afrika Mashariki, msichana mdogo alifanya kazi za shambanin pamoja na mamake. Msichana huyu aliitwa Wangari. {% include textcol_end.html %}

In a village on the slopes of Mount Kenya in East Africa, a little girl worked in the fields with her mother. Her name was Wangari.

Katika kijiji kimoja kwenye mteremko wa Mlima Kenya, katika Afrika Mashariki, msichana mdogo alifanya kazi za shambanin pamoja na mamake. Msichana huyu aliitwa Wangari.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="02" prev="01" next="03" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="03" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Wangari alipenda kufanya kazi za nje. Alitumia jembe kulima katika bustani ya familia yake. Kisha alipanda mbegu ndogo katika ardhi yenye joto. {% include textcol_end.html %}

Wangari loved being outside. In her family's food garden she broke up the soil with her machete. She pressed tiny seeds into the warm earth.

Wangari alipenda kufanya kazi za nje. Alitumia jembe kulima katika bustani ya familia yake. Kisha alipanda mbegu ndogo katika ardhi yenye joto.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="03" prev="02" next="04" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="04" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Alipenda majira ya jioni, wakati jua lilipotua. Giza lilipozidi na kuwa vigumu kuona mimea tena, Wangari alifahamu wakati wa kwenda nyumbani uliwadia. Angefuata kijia chembamba kilichopita wanjani huku akivuka mito akielekea nyumbani. {% include textcol_end.html %}

Her favourite time of day was just after sunset. When it got too dark to see the plants, Wangari knew it was time to go home. She would follow the narrow paths through the fields, crossing rivers as she went.

Alipenda majira ya jioni, wakati jua lilipotua. Giza lilipozidi na kuwa vigumu kuona mimea tena, Wangari alifahamu wakati wa kwenda nyumbani uliwadia. Angefuata kijia chembamba kilichopita wanjani huku akivuka mito akielekea nyumbani.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="04" prev="03" next="05" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="05" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Wangari alikuwa mtoto mwerevu na alikuwa na hamu ya kwenda shule. Lakini, wazazi wake walitaka awasaidie nyumbani. Alipofika umri wa miaka saba, kakake aliwashawishi wazazi wake kumwacha aende shule. {% include textcol_end.html %}

Wangari was a clever child and couldn't wait to go to school. But her mother and father wanted her to stay and help them at home. When she was seven years old, her big brother persuaded her parents to let her go to school.

Wangari alikuwa mtoto mwerevu na alikuwa na hamu ya kwenda shule. Lakini, wazazi wake walitaka awasaidie nyumbani. Alipofika umri wa miaka saba, kakake aliwashawishi wazazi wake kumwacha aende shule.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="05" prev="04" next="06" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="06" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Alipenda kusoma! Wangari alijifunza zaidi na zaidi kwa kila kitabu alichosoma. Alifanya vyema sana shuleni hadi akaalikwa kwenda Amerika kuendeleza masoma yake huko. Wangari alichangamshwa sana na nafasi hii! Alipenda kujua ulimwengu zaidi. {% include textcol_end.html %}

She liked to learn! Wangari learnt more and more with every book she read. She did so well at school that she was invited to study in the United States of America. Wangari was excited! She wanted to know more about the world.

Alipenda kusoma! Wangari alijifunza zaidi na zaidi kwa kila kitabu alichosoma. Alifanya vyema sana shuleni hadi akaalikwa kwenda Amerika kuendeleza masoma yake huko. Wangari alichangamshwa sana na nafasi hii! Alipenda kujua ulimwengu zaidi.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="06" prev="05" next="07" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="07" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Alipokuwa chuoni huko Amerika, Wangari alijifunza mengi mapya. Alisomea mimea na jinsi inavyokua. Na alikumbuka maisha yake yalivyokuwa utotoni: kucheza michezo na nduguze katika kivuli cha miti maridadi ya misitu ya Kenya. {% include textcol_end.html %}

At the American university Wangari learnt many new things. She studied plants and how they grow. And she remembered how she grew: playing games with her brothers in the shade of the trees in the beautiful Kenyan forests.

Alipokuwa chuoni huko Amerika, Wangari alijifunza mengi mapya. Alisomea mimea na jinsi inavyokua. Na alikumbuka maisha yake yalivyokuwa utotoni: kucheza michezo na nduguze katika kivuli cha miti maridadi ya misitu ya Kenya.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="07" prev="06" next="08" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="08" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Jinsi alivyojifunza ndivyo alivyofahamu kuwa aliwapenda watu wa Kenya. Alitaka wafurahi na wawe wenye uhuru. Jinsi alivyojifunza ndivyo alikumbuka kwao nyumbani Afrika. {% include textcol_end.html %}

The more she learnt, the more she realised that she loved the people of Kenya. She wanted them to be happy and free. The more she learnt, the more she remembered her African home.

Jinsi alivyojifunza ndivyo alivyofahamu kuwa aliwapenda watu wa Kenya. Alitaka wafurahi na wawe wenye uhuru. Jinsi alivyojifunza ndivyo alikumbuka kwao nyumbani Afrika.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="08" prev="07" next="09" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="09" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Alipokamilisha masomo yake, alirudi Kenya. Lakini nchi yake ilikuwa imebadilika. Kulikuwa na mashamba makubwa kila upande. Wanawake hawakupata kuni za kuwasha moto wa kupika. Watu walikuwa maskini na watoto walikuwa na njaa. {% include textcol_end.html %}

When she had finished her studies, she returned to Kenya. But her country had changed. Huge farms stretched across the land. Women had no wood to make cooking fires. The people were poor and the children were hungry.

Alipokamilisha masomo yake, alirudi Kenya. Lakini nchi yake ilikuwa imebadilika. Kulikuwa na mashamba makubwa kila upande. Wanawake hawakupata kuni za kuwasha moto wa kupika. Watu walikuwa maskini na watoto walikuwa na njaa.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="09" prev="08" next="10" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="10" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Wangari alijua la kufanya. Aliwafunza wanawake jinsi ya kupanda miti kutokana na miche. Wanawake waliuza ile miti na pesa walizopata walizitumia kwa mahitaji ya familia zao. Wanawake walifurahi. Wangari alikuwa amewasaidie kuwa wenye nguvu na uwezo. {% include textcol_end.html %}

Wangari knew what to do. She taught the women how to plant trees from seeds. The women sold the trees and used the money to look after their families. The women were very happy. Wangari had helped them to feel powerful and strong.

Wangari alijua la kufanya. Aliwafunza wanawake jinsi ya kupanda miti kutokana na miche. Wanawake waliuza ile miti na pesa walizopata walizitumia kwa mahitaji ya familia zao. Wanawake walifurahi. Wangari alikuwa amewasaidie kuwa wenye nguvu na uwezo.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="10" prev="09" next="11" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="11" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Muda ulipopita, miti mipya ilikuwa misitu, na mito ilianza tena kupitisha maji. Ujumbe wa Wangari ulienea Africa nzima. Leo, mamilioni ya miti imemea kutokana na mbegu za Wangari. {% include textcol_end.html %}

As time passed, the new trees grew into forests, and the rivers started flowing again. Wangari's message spread across Africa. Today, millions of trees have grown from Wangari's seeds.

Muda ulipopita, miti mipya ilikuwa misitu, na mito ilianza tena kupitisha maji. Ujumbe wa Wangari ulienea Africa nzima. Leo, mamilioni ya miti imemea kutokana na mbegu za Wangari.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="11" prev="10" next="12" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="12" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Wangari alikuwa amefanya bidii. Watu ulimwengu mzima walifahamu hili na wakampa tuzo. Tuzo hili linaitwa, 'Nobel Peace Prize.' Na alikuwa mwanamke wa Kiafrika wa kwanza kupokea tuzo hilo. {% include textcol_end.html %}

Wangari had worked hard. People all over the world took notice, and gave her a famous prize. It is called the Nobel Peace Prize, and she was the first African woman ever to receive it.

Wangari alikuwa amefanya bidii. Watu ulimwengu mzima walifahamu hili na wakampa tuzo. Tuzo hili linaitwa, 'Nobel Peace Prize.' Na alikuwa mwanamke wa Kiafrika wa kwanza kupokea tuzo hilo.

{% include audiocontrols.html index="12" prev="11" next="13" %}
{% include picturecol.html index="13" %} {% include textcol_start.html %} Wangari aliaga dunia mwaka 2011, lakini tunaweza kumfikiria kila tunapouona mti wa kupendeza. {% include textcol_end.html %}

Wangari died in 2011, but we can think of her every time we see a beautiful tree.

Wangari aliaga dunia mwaka 2011, lakini tunaweza kumfikiria kila tunapouona mti wa kupendeza.
