Fiiri xoolaha Look at the animals

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Anwar Mohamed Dirie

Somali

Level 1

Fiiri xoolaha

Look at the animals.

Tazama wanyama.

Sacu waxa uu u ciyaa, "Moo."

The cow says, "Moo."

Ng'ombe anasema, "Moo."

Ri'du waxa ay u cidaa, "Meh, meh."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

Mbuzi anasema, "Me, me."

Farasku waxa uu u ciyaa, "Neigh."

The horse says, "Neigh."

Farasi anasema, "Nei."

Doofaarku waxa uu u ciyaa, "Grunt."

The pig says, "Grunt."

Nguruwe anasema, "Ghuranti."

Digaagaddu waxa ay u cidaa, "Cluck."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

Kuku anasema ,"Ko,ko."

Eygu waxa uu u ciyaa, "Woof."

The dog says, "Woof."

Mbwa anasema, "Bwe."

Beerwaluhu waxa uu yiraahdaa, "Shhh!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Mkulima anasema,"Kimya."

Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Anwar Mohamed Dirie
