Maxaad samaynaysaa? What are you doing?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Abdi Muse

Somali

Level 1

Waan heesayaa.

I am singing.

Ninaimba.

Gacanta ayay u haadinaysaa.

She is waving.

Anapunga mkono.

Waan sacbinayaa.

I am clapping.

Ninapiga makofi.

Waay jimicsanaysaa.

She is stretching.

Anajinyoosha.

Wuu u yeedhayaa.

He is calling.

Anaita.

Waan ka jawaabayaa.

I am answering.

Ninaitika.

Way dhagaysanaysaa.

She is listening.

Anasikiliza.

Maxaad samaynaysaa?

What are you doing?

Wewe unafanya nini?

Written by: Nina Orange
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Abdi Muse
Language: Somali
Level 1
