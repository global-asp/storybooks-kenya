Dab Fire

Deborah Namugosa, Beatres Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Ali Abdi Birkh

Somali

Level 1

Fiiri dabka!

Look, fire!

Angalia, moto!

Dabka wuu gubaa.

Fire burns.

Moto unachoma.

Dabka wuu kariyaa.

Fire cooks.

Moto unatumika kupika.

Dabka waxuu nasiiyaa qandac.

Fire gives warmth.

Moto unaleta joto.

Dabka waxuu nasiiyaa iftiin.

Fire gives light.

Moto unaleta mwanga.

Fiiri dabka!

Look, fire!

Angalia, moto!

Dabka waa layaab.

Fire is wonderful.

Moto ni wa ajabu.

Waana mid awood leh.

And it is powerful.

Na una nguvu sana.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatres Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Ali Abdi Birkh
Language: Somali
Level 1
