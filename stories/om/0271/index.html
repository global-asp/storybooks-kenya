Lama. Two

Carole Bloch

Richard MacIntosh

Demoze Degefa 

Oromo

Level 1

Harka xixiqqo lama qabachuu.

Two little hands to hold.

Mikono miwili midogo ya kushika.

Mila xixixqqo lama warukktuf.

Two little feet to kick.

Miguu miwili midogo kupiga teke.

Eja xixiqqo lama ilaaluf.

Two little eyes to see.

Macho mawili madogo kuona.

Gurra xixiqqo lama dhageefachuuf.

Two little ears to hear.

Masikio mawili madogo kusikia.

Akasuma harka lama walhamchuuf.

And two loving arms to HUG!

Na mikono miwili KUKUMBATIA kwa upendo.

Written by: Carole Bloch
Illustrated by: Richard MacIntosh
Translated by: Demoze Degefa 
Language: Oromo
Level 1
