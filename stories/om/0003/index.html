Offata mana Barmusaa. School clothes Sare za shule

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter

Demoze Degefa

Oromo

Level 1

Wandaboon kuni dheeraa dha.

This dress is long.

Gauni hili ni refu.

Shurrabin kuni guuddaa dha.

This jersey is big.

Sweta hii ni kubwa.

Boorsaan kuni guuddaa dha.

This bag is big.

Mkoba huu ni mkubwa.

Saqqiin kuni…

This belt is...

Mkanda huu ni...

Gonfeen kuni xiqqaa dha.

This hat is small.

Kofia hii ni ndogo.

Shurrabin milaa kuni gabaabaadha.

The socks are short.

Soksi hizi ni fupi.

Kopheen kuni haara’aa.

But these shoes are new.

Lakini viatu hivi ni vipya.

Koppen kuni haalan ta’aa.

... they fit well.

...vinanitosha vizuri.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Demoze Degefa
Language: Oromo
Level 1
