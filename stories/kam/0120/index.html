Nzwĩĩ Hair

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath

Anna Kula

Kikamba

Level 1

Thuli ena nzwĩĩ nguvĩ.

Thuli has short hair.

Thuli ana nywele fupi.

Anna ena nzwĩĩ ndaasa.

Anna has long hair.

Anna ana nywele ndefu.

Kathy nĩwe wĩna Nzwĩĩ ndaasa kwĩ angĩ.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Kate ana nywele ndefu kuliko wote.

Zama nũsukĩte nzwĩĩ yake.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Zama amesuka nywele zake.

Nau ena kĩng’ee.

Baba has a beard.

Baba ana ndevu.

Nzanele nũsanũĩte nzwĩĩ yake.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Zanele amechana nywele zake.

Thambo nũtilĩte nzwĩĩ yake.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Thabo amenyoa nywele zake.

Themba nĩwenzete nzwĩĩ yake.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Themba umenyoa kipara.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath
Translated by: Anna Kula
Language: Kikamba
Level 1
