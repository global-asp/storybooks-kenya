Ngua sya sukulu School clothes Sare za shule

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini

Stephen Musyoka

Kikamba

Level 1

Ilinda yii ni yaasa.

This dress is long.

Gauni hili ni refu.

Vulana ii ni nene.

This jersey is big.

Sweta hii ni kubwa.

Muvuko uu ni munene.

This bag is big.

Mkoba huu ni mkubwa.

Musivi uu...........

This belt is...

Mkanda huu ni …

Ngovia ii ni nini.

This hat is small.

Kofia hii ni ndogo.

Sokisi ni nguvi.

The socks are short.

Soksi hizi ni fupi.

Na syaatu ii ni nzau.

But these shoes are new.

Lakini viatu hivi ni vipya.

...syaania nesa.

... they fit well.

… vinanitosha vizuri.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini
Translated by: Stephen Musyoka
Language: Kikamba
Level 1
