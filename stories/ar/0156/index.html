التِّمْسَاحُ الجَائِعُ The hungry crocodile

Christian G.

Wiehan de Jager

Hanadi Traifeh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk

Mashael Muhanna

Arabic

Level 1

فِي يَوْمٍ مِنَ الأَيَّامِ، كَانَ هُنَالِكَ تِمْسَاحٌ جَائِعٌ.

Once there was a very hungry crocodile.

Wakati mmoja palikuwa na mamba mwenye njaa.

كَانَ يَبْحَثُ عَنِ الطَّعَامِ بِبُطْءٍ وَهُدُوءٍ. وَفَجْأَةً…

He searched for food slowly and quietly. And then...

Alitafuta chakula pole pole tena kimya. Halafu...

طجججج!!! قَفَزَ التِّمْسَاحُ!

POW!!! The crocodile strikes!

POO!!! Mamba anashambulia!

لَمْ يَعُدْ التِّمْسَاحُ جَائِعاً بَعْدَ ذَلِكَ، بَلْ أَصْبَحَ سَعِيدًا.

After that he is no longer hungry, and he is happy.

Baada ya hiyo hana njaa tena,na anafurahi.

إِلَى أَنْ يَجُوعَ مَرَّةً أُخْرَى.

Until he gets hungry again.

Hadi anapohisi njaa tena.

Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Hanadi Traifeh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk
Read by: Mashael Muhanna
