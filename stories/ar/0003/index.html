مَلَابِسُ المَدْرَسَةِ School clothes

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini

Heba Tesheh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk

Mashael Muhanna

Arabic

Level 1

هَذَا الفُسْتَانُ طَوِيلٌ.

This dress is long.

Rinda hili ni refu.

هَذِهِ الكَنْزَةُ كَبيرَةٌ.

This jersey is big.

Sweta hii ni kubwa.

هَذِهِ الحَقيبَةُ كَبيرَةٌ.

This bag is big.

Mkoba huu ni mkubwa.

هَذَا الحِزامُ…

This belt is...

Mshipi huu...

هَذِهِ القُبَّعَةُ صَغِيرَةٌ.

This hat is small.

Kofia hii ni ndogo.

هَذِهِ الجَوَارِبُ قَصِيرَةٌ.

The socks are short.

Sokisi hizi ni fupi.

لَكِنَّ هَذَا الحِذَاءَ جَديدٌ.

But these shoes are new.

Lakini viatu hivi ni vipya.

إِنَّهُ مُنَاسِبٌ جِدًّا…

... they fit well.

....vinanitosha vizuri.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini
Translated by: Heba Tesheh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk
Read by: Mashael Muhanna
Language: Arabic
Level 1
