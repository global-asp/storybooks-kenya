Sandy Campbell

Darshan Soni

English

Level 1

Look at the animals.

Look at the animals.

Tazama wanyama.

The cow says, "Moo."

The cow says, "Moo."

Ng'ombe anasema, "Moo."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

Mbuzi anasema, "Me, me."

The horse says, "Neigh."

The horse says, "Neigh."

Farasi anasema, "Nei."

The pig says, "Grunt."

The pig says, "Grunt."

Nguruwe anasema, "Ghuranti."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

Kuku anasema ,"Ko,ko."

The dog says, "Woof."

The dog says, "Woof."

Mbwa anasema, "Bwe."

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Mkulima anasema,"Kimya."
