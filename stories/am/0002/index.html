እንስሳቱን ተመልከቱ Look at the animals Tazama wanyama

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

ፋሲል አሠፋ (Fasil Assefa)

Amharic

Level 1

እንስሳቱን ተመልከቷቸው።

Look at the animals.

Tazama wanyama.

ላሚቷ ‹‹እምቧ›› ትላለች።

The cow says, "Moo."

Ng'ombe anasema, "Moo."

ፍየሏ ደግሞ ‹‹ሚሂሂሂ ……..›› ትላለች።

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

Mbuzi anasema, "Mee, mee."

ፈረሱም እያስካካ ነው።

The horse says, "Neigh."

Farasi anasema, "Nei."

አሳማውም ያጉረመርማል።

The pig says, "Grunt."

Nguruwe anasema, "Ghuranti."

ዶሮዋ ደግሞ ታስካካለች።

The chicken says, "Cluck."

Kuku anasema, "Ko, ko, ko."

ውሻው ‹‹ዋው…ዋው…ዋው…›› እያለ ይጮሀል።

The dog says, "Woof."

Mbwa anasema, "Bwe, bwe."

ገበሬው ደግሞ ዝም እንዲሉ ‹‹እሽሽሽሽ›› እያለ ድምፅ ያሰማል።

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Mkulima anasema,"Nyamaza!"

Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: ፋሲል አሠፋ (Fasil Assefa)
