Enviri Hair Nywele

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter

Rev Joseph M Katabaro

Luganda

Level 1

Babirye alina enviiri ennyimpi.

Thuli has short hair.

Thuli ana nywele fupi.

Nakato alina enviiri empanvu.

Anna has long hair.

Anna ana nywele ndefu.

Nambi y’alina enviiri ezisinga obuwanvu.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Cathy ana nywele ndefu kuliko wote.

Birungi asibye enviiri.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Zama amesuka nywele zake.

Musa alina ekirevu.

Baba has a beard.

Baba ana ndevu.

Nakku asaniridde enviiri ze.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Zanele amechana nywele zake.

Kato asazeeko enviiri ze.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Thabo amenyoa nywele zake.

Kapere amweddeko enviiri ze zonna.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Themba amenyoa kipara.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Rev Joseph M Katabaro
Language: Luganda
Level 1
Source: Hair from African Storybook
