Akatabo k'obudde Weather book Kitabu cha hali ya hewa

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall

Mwesigwa Joshua Waswa

Luganda

Level 1

Obudde bwa mukalwe.

It is dry.

Hakuna mvua.

Omusana gwaka nnyo.

The sun is hot.

Jua linang'aa.

Obudde bwa kibuyaga.

It is windy.

Kuna upepo.

Obudde bwa kidde.

It is cloudy.

Kuna mawingu.

Obudde bunnyogovu.

It is cold.

Kuna baridi.

Enkuba etonnya.

It is raining.

Mvua inanyesha.

Eggulu libwatuka.

There is thunder.

Kuna ngurumo za radi.

Ndaba Musoke.

I see a rainbow.

Naona upinde wa mvua.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall
Translated by: Mwesigwa Joshua Waswa
Language: Luganda
Level 1
