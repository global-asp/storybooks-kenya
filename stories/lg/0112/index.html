Omubiri gwange My body

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini

Rev Joseph M Katabaro

Luganda

Level 1

Nsobola okudduka nagwo.

I can run with it.

Ninaweza kukimbia nayo.

Nsobola okubuuka nagwo.

I can jump with it.

Ninaweza kuruka nayo.

Nsobola okuzina nagwo.

I can dance with it.

Ninaweza kucheza nayo.

Nsobola okuwuga nagwo.

I can swim with it.

Ninaweza kuogelea nayo.

Nsobola okubuuka omuguwa nagwo.

I can skip with it.

Ninaweza kupiga nayo.

Nsobola okugusambisa omupiira.

I can kick with it.

Ninaweza kutoroka nayo.

Nsobola okubulawo nagwo.

I can escape with it.

Ninaweza kuepa nayo.

Naye sisobola kubuuka mu bbanga nagwo.

But I can never fly with it.

Lakini siwezi kupaa angani nayo.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini
Translated by: Rev Joseph M Katabaro
Language: Luganda
Level 1
Source: My body from African Storybook
