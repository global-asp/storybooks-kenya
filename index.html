Storybooks Kenya

Storybooks Kenya is designed specifically for educators and makes 40 stories from the African Storybook available in English and Swahili, as well as the major national, immigrant and refugee languages of Kenya (with text and audio in Gikuyu, Gujarati, Somali, Afan Oromo, and Afan Oromo among others).
