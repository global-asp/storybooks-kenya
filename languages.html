Languages

The following languages are currently included in the scope of the Storybooks Kenya project:

  • Amharic
  • Arabic
  • Embu / Kîembu
  • English
  • Gujarati
  • Ekegusii
  • Kamba / Kikamba
  • Gikuyu / Kikuyu
  • Kalenjin
  • Luganda
  • Olukhayo
  • Luo / Dholuo
  • Luhya / Oluluhya
  • Pokomo / Kipfokomo
  • Maa / Maasai
  • Meru / Kimîîru
  • Giryama / Kigiryama
  • Oromo / Afaan Oromo
  • Rendille
  • Samburu
  • Somali
  • Swahili / Kiswahili
  • Turkana / Ng‘aturkana
We are always looking for translators! If you speak one of the above languages and would like to help us translate stories, please contact us at globalafricanstorybook [at] gmail [dot] com
